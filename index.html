Fun Facts About Japan
Japan's population (as of September 27, 2023): 123,196,770

The main language spoken is Japanese. There are three types of phoenetic Japanese: Kanji, Hiragana and Katakana. Kanji are Chinese characters used to write Japanese while Hiragana and Katakana are both syllabary.
